Ateez

Ateez is a South Korean boy band formed by KQ Entertainment . The group consists of eight members: Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung and Jongho. They debuted on October 24, 2018, with the extended play (EP) Treasure EP.1: All to Zero. The group’s name, ATEEZ, comes from “A TEEnage Z”, meaning “everything about teenagers”. Since the beginning of their debut, the group has received attention for their performances and ongoing storyline that unfolds through their music videos.
